Or, to return again to the past, take as another instance the ancient Greeks. Do you think that Greek art ever tells us what the Greek people are like? Do you believe that the Athenian women were like the stately dignified figures of the Parthenon frieze, or like those marvellous goddesses that sit in the triangular pediment? who sat in the triangular pediments of the same building? If you judge from the art, they certainly were so.
