Our Luciens de Rubempré, our Rastignacs, and De Marsays made their first appearance in the Comédie Humaine. We are merely carrying out, with footnotes and unnecessary additions, the whim or fancy of a great novelist. I once asked a lady, who knew Thackeray intimately, whether he had had any model for Becky Sharp. She told me that Becky was an invention, but that the idea of the character had been in part suggested by a governess who lived in the neighbourhood of Kensington Square and was the companion of a very selfish old la and rich old woman. I had asked her enquired what became of the governess, and she replied that, oddly enough, some time years after the appearance of "Vanity Fair," the governess ran away with the nephew of the lady with whom she was living, and for a short time made a great splash in society quite in Mrs Rawdon Crawley's method . Ultimately she came to grief, her husband received a Colonial appointment, and she disappeared to the Continent, andand used to be occasionally seen at Monte Carlo and other gambling places.
